Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective May 8, 2026

Zuckerman Rentals, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates this website at thewondervalleyranch.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit the site or send us an inquiry, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

We collect information you choose to provide through our inquiry forms — typically your name, email address, phone number (optional), preferred property, travel dates, and any message you include. We do not require you to create an account.

We also collect limited, anonymous traffic information automatically — pages visited, referring site, browser type, and approximate location — through Vercel's first-party analytics. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and confirm availability and pricing
  • To send you property details you've asked about
  • To improve the site based on aggregate, anonymous traffic patterns

We do not use your information to send marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional messages unless you specifically ask us to.

How it's stored and shared

Inquiry submissions are handled by our own backend and delivered to our email inbox via Resend, an email delivery service. Resend's privacy practices are described at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as needed to operate the site (e.g., our email and form-handling providers) or as required by law.

Cookies and tracking

The site uses Vercel's first-party analytics, which sets a single anonymous cookie to count unique visitors. We do not use third-party advertising trackers, retargeting pixels, or social-media tracking widgets.

Your rights

If you are a resident of California, the European Union, or another jurisdiction with applicable privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete it
  • Opt out of any future communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at zuckermanhq@gmail.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Retention

We retain inquiry data for as long as is reasonably necessary to respond to you and follow up. If your inquiry doesn't lead to a booking, we typically delete or archive it within 24 months. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

Children

The site is intended for adults considering a vacation rental. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will be highlighted on the home page for at least 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Email zuckermanhq@gmail.com.
